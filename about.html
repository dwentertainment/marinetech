INSTITUTE OF MARINE AND


TECHNOLOGY


INSTITUTE OF MARINE AND TECHNOLOGY is a private educational instituation located within Eket and Etina Local Government Areas of Akwa Ibom State. As a private academic institustion we undertake the responsibility of providing our students exceptional opportunities in vocational and maritime business fields.
We design our programmes to blend a tradition of morden academic achivement with emphasis on exoerimental learing.
Our curriculum provides the skills needed to succeed in a competitive global business enviroment, utilizing a combination of field work, pratical, skill acquistion, information technology system, research exercise and lectures.Each of our courses is designed to give you direct experice with challenges faced by today business professionals, sharpening your ability to solve problem and creat a career of sound enterpreneurship. We have a stable learning enviroment where student work as a family, strike a balance to meet competitive requirement both in theiry and pratical. Our goal is substantially different with a familiar academic culture.Our intense, intimate and interactive academic community has all the advantage you exepct from a good maritime business enviroment

.a) vide professional training that will meet the obviously yearning need for qualified mariners
.b) To provide facilitTo proies for physical and mentaly fitness in order to boost academic excellence.
