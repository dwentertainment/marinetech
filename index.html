Welcome To IMT NIGERIA

INSTITUTE OF MARINE AND TECHNOLOGY is a private educational instituation located within Eket and Etinan Local Government Areas of Akwa Ibom State. As a private academic institustion we undertake the responsibility of providing our students exceptional opportunities in vocational and maritime business fields. We design our programmes to blend a tradition of morden academic achivement with emphasis on experimental learing.
